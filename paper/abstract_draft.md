# Abstract Draft

We present Odyssey, a research prototype for risk-aware intrusion detection under post-quantum transition conditions. The framework combines a classical attack predictor, a small simulator-based quantum uncertainty head, a transparent crypto-transition fragility score, and temporal consistency regularization. We evaluate the proposed method on a fully reproducible synthetic benchmark and a public IDS adapter with documented augmentation assumptions. Results are reported with calibration, rare-attack sensitivity, runtime, and seed-stability analyses to avoid overclaiming from isolated runs.
